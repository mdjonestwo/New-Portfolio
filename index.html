Who is Michael Jones?

A polymath who’s curiosity allowed him to practice multiple talents, skills and fields of study, Michael D. Jones II is a man who loves to learn. He is the author and illustrator of several educational coloring books, a recording musician who’s performed throughout the country and released multiple albums, an operations coordinator for an educational gardening organization for youth and is currently enrolled in a software development bootcamp at the University of New Hampshire. He lives a wonderful life with his wife and pit bull in Durham, North Carolin while working at an organic food distribution center as a fulfillment technician. Michael is dedicated to solving humanitarian and environmental problems to serve God and his community.
